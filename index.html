175. calibrate

IPA 音标：/ˈkæl.ə.breɪt/

拼读：KAL-uh-brayt

中文：校准；使标准化

英文：to set, adjust, or standardize

例句发音：

SAT例句：
The engineer had to calibrate the instrument before the experiment began.

中文翻译：
实验开始前，工程师必须校准仪器。

💡 SAT重点词汇老师讲解

SAT中常用于科学、技术或抽象语境，表示把标准调准确。

Day 7 SAT Reading｜A Play in Chaos

At a school arts festival, the theater club tried to present a play about a legendary bard. Before the show, the director had to calibrate the lights, catalog the props, and check the canvas backdrop. The room was capacious, but the mood quickly turned to chaos when a capricious actor threatened to capitulate and quit. The student leader, though usually bashful, showed remarkable candor. She did not chide the actor harshly or chastise him in public. Instead, she used a calm cadence to explain why the performance mattered. Her words helped catalyze a change in the group. A callous critic had written a caustic review before even seeing the play, but the cast refused to be discouraged. Their camaraderie grew stronger. The choreography captivated the audience, and even the critic stopped trying to carp about minor flaws. In the end, the show became a cherished chronicle of how teamwork can turn disorder into art.

Main Idea 主旨句题

Q. Which sentence best states the main idea of the passage?

点击显示答案

A calm and honest leader helps a group overcome chaos and create a successful artistic performance.
