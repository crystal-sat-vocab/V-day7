二、5–10分钟小测

1. A convincing and logical argument is ____.

  1. capricious
  2. cogent
  3. clandestine
  4. chronic
显示答案

B. cogent

2. To criticize someone severely is to ____ them.

  1. castigate
  2. coalesce
  3. comply
  4. circumvent
显示答案

A. castigate

3. A secret meeting may be described as ____.

  1. colloquial
  2. clandestine
  3. cerebral
  4. commensurate
显示答案

B. clandestine

4. A person who is calm under pressure is ____.

  1. composed
  2. caustic
  3. complacent
  4. capricious
显示答案

A. composed

5. Something that causes major change is a ____.

  1. cliché
  2. catalyst
  3. censure
  4. catharsis
显示答案

B. catalyst

6. To avoid a rule by finding a way around it is to ____ it.

  1. comply
  2. coalesce
  3. circumvent
  4. censure
显示答案

C. circumvent

7. A long-lasting health problem is ____.

  1. chronic
  2. charismatic
  3. categorical
  4. cogent
显示答案

A. chronic

8. Secret dishonest cooperation is ____.

  1. clemency
  2. collusion
  3. coercion
  4. chagrin
显示答案

B. collusion

A more circumspect member warned that the group should not become complacent or try to circumvent school rules. complacent
