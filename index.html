二、5–10分钟小测

1. A coach may ____ a machine before a race so that it gives accurate readings.

  1. carouse
  2. calibrate
  3. carp
  4. chide
显示答案

B. calibrate

2. Someone who is ____ often changes plans for no clear reason.

  1. capacious
  2. caustic
  3. capricious
  4. cerebral
显示答案

C. capricious

3. A speech full of honesty and openness shows ____.

  1. candor
  2. calumny
  3. chaos
  4. censure
显示答案

A. candor

4. If a performance can ____ the audience, it can hold their attention.

  1. captivate
  2. capitulate
  3. catalog
  4. cavort
显示答案

A. captivate

5. A route that is long and indirect is best described as ____.

  1. chronological
  2. circuitous
  3. canny
  4. caustic
显示答案

B. circuitous

6. A cruel rumor spread to damage someone’s reputation is ____.

  1. camaraderie
  2. canvas
  3. calumny
  4. caucus
显示答案

C. calumny

7. A very intellectual novel may be called ____.

  1. cerebral
  2. callous
  3. capacious
  4. capricious
显示答案

A. cerebral

8. If a country agrees to surrender, it will ____.

  1. capitulate
  2. carouse
  3. choreograph
  4. chastise
显示答案

A. capitulate

四、阅读找词任务

When the city museum began preparing its new dance exhibit, the curator first worked to catalog every costume and photograph in chronological order. Her canny assistant noticed that the original plan for the room was too circuitous, forcing visitors to wander in circles. After a long caucus with the design team, they chose a more direct layout. The choreographer they invited to advise on the exhibit was known for work so cerebral that some visitors found it difficult, but the curator believed the final display would still captivate the public.

1. Which word in the passage means “organized as a list”?

显示答案

catalog

2. Which word means “wise and careful”?

显示答案

canny

3. Which word means “arranged by time”?

显示答案

chronological

4. Which word means “indirect or roundabout”?

显示答案

circuitous

5. Which word means “a formal meeting for discussion”?

显示答案

caucus

6. Which word means “intellectual”?

显示答案

cerebral

7. Which word means “to attract strongly”?

显示答案

captivate
